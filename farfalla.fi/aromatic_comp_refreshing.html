Aromatic Compositions

Refreshing and uplifting...

Citrus Garden
A blend containing blood orange, lime, green mandarin, peppermint and lemon. This fruity-fresh, slightly minty aroma brings cheerfulness and brightness into our living rooms. It is refreshing, revitalising, energising and lifts the spirit. Makes an ideal gift.

Emerald Forest
A blend including douglas fir, palmarosa, ravintsara, silver fir. A mystical and cheerful woodland blend with a fresh, bright scent, like a forest breeze in summer. All the oils in this blend have a very beneficial effect on the respiratory tract. Emerald Forest is also suitable for cleansing and vitalising the air.

Energy
A blend including munuka, conifers, peppermint and silver fir. The oils from the conifer and myrtle families have an energising, stimulating and fortifying effect on the body. We recommend the use of this blend particularly as a prevention and as a pick-me-up. It is sensuous and heart warming.

Heart & Soul
A blend including copaiba, clove, sandalwood, styrax and vanilla. A spicy mix for emotional people with a burning passion. The base note is Indian cardamom, fiery pepper, sandalwood and patchouli and is complimented, as a top note, by the tangy, zesty characteristics of litsea cubeba and orange.
It is stimulating and invigorating.
