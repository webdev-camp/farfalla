Aroma Sprays

Aromasprayt sopivat hyvin ilman raikastamisen huoneessa, neutraloimaan epämiellyttäviä hajuja tai luomaan miellyttävän tuoksuvan tunnelman huoneeseen tai tilaan. Aroma sprayt sisältävät luonnollisia eteerisiä öljyjä ja ovat siksi luonnollisia , mutta tehokkaita. Niitä voi käyttää kotona, wc.ssä, toimistoissa, autossa, hoitohuoneissa tai sairaaloissa.

Ambiance

Composition: a flowery, fresh, stimulating scent to set the mood for a calming environment. Uses: living areas, offices, reception areas, waiting rooms. Contents: bergamot, orange*, lemon*, rose geranium, peppermint.

Atmosphere

Composition: a fresh, tangy scent, strongly purifying and an aid to concentration. Uses: to neutralise odours and to use as air freshener in rooms and vehicles. Contents: eucalyptus*, clove, neroli, lemon*, frankincense, lemongrass*.

Fresh

Composition: fresh, fruity, lemon touch. For refreshing the home and when travelling, in the car and hotel room. Boosts concentration when driving and keeps one awake. Contents: blood orange*, bergamot*, peppermint

Tuscany

Composition: a fruity, woody scent, which refreshes and stimulates the senses. Uses: It can be used as an air freshener, to stimulate and cleanse rooms, conference halls and offices. Contents: bergamot, chamomile, lavender*, rose geranium, lemon*, cypress*.

Vanilla-Mandarine

Composition: soft and fruity, mellow. This refreshing spray is a comforter for the soul and can be used everywhere as pleased. For the home, when travelling, in the childrens room etc.
