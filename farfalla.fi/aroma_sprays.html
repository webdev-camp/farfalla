Aroma Sprays

Ideal to refresh the air in a room, neutralise odours or simply to create a scented environment. For a breath of fresh air, our Aroma Sprays with their natural perfumes are highly effective. For use in: homes, offices, toilets and bathrooms, cars, therapy rooms, waiting rooms, hositals.


Ambiance
Composition: a flowery, fresh, stimulating scent to set the mood for a calming environment.
Uses: living areas, offices, reception areas, waiting rooms.
Contents: bergamot, orange*, lemon*, rose geranium, peppermint.

Atmosphere
Composition: a fresh, tangy scent, strongly purifying and an aid to concentration.
Uses: to neutralise odours and to use as air freshener in rooms and vehicles.
Contents: eucalyptus*, clove, neroli, lemon*, frankincense, lemongrass*.

Fresh
Composition: fresh, fruity, lemon touch. For refreshing the home and when travelling, in the car and hotel room. Boosts concentration when driving and keeps one awake.
Contents: blood orange*, bergamot*, peppermint

Tuscany
 Composition: a fruity, woody scent, which refreshes and stimulates the senses.
Uses: It can be used as an air freshener, to stimulate and cleanse rooms, conference halls and offices.
Contents: bergamot, chamomile, lavender*, rose geranium, lemon*, cypress*.

Vanilla-Mandarine
Composition: soft and fruity, mellow. This refreshing spray is a comforter for the soul and can be used everywhere as pleased. For the home, when travelling, in the childrens room etc.
