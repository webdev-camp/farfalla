Farfalla Essentials Ltd. is a Swiss company that was established more than 20 years ago and has been committed to natural aromatherapy and wellness or SPA-products since the beginning. The main products are natural essential oils, mostly certified organic, oil compositions, certified natural cosmetics, natural perfumes, Aroma Sprays, bubble baths, aromatic sea salt and algea baths, shower gels and natural shampoos care and a wide assortment of basic carrier oils and frangrances body and massage oils. Farfallas natural perfumes are genuine and natural and carry the BDIH label. However we go even further: 98 % of the substance stem from controlled organic crop. As a base Farfalla uses alcohol and flower water specially produced in organic quality. No animal testing.
