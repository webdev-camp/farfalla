Aromatic Compositions

Protective, cleansing, focusing...

Atmosphere
A blend including eucalyptus, frankincense, lemon and lemongrass. A fresh, lemony, slightly tart scent. We created this blend for two main reasons. Firstly to cleanse the air of unpleasant odours, and secondly to neutralise negative vibrations.

Aura
Includes angelica, geranium, scotch pine, neroli, frankincense. A clear, fresh and balancing «heart-blend». This mix is aimed particularly at those who need protection, purification and clarity. It is also available as an eau de parfum and a bubble bath.

Concentration
A blend with basil, lemon balm, peppermint and rosemary. The synergy of this blend contains essential oils mainly from Mediterranean herbs. Their effect is stimulating, provides clarity to the mind. Ideal for concentrated work and before sitting an exam. A tangy and refreshing scent.
