Aromatic Compositions

Sensuous, emotional...

Romantic Night
Includes jasmine, centifolia rose, rose geranium,
sandalwood, tuberose and ylang ylang. This blend can best be described as oriental and flowery, soft and gentle. For sensuous, tender moments. Cajoles the heart and wakes the senses.

Taj Mahal
With orange, jasmine, patchouli, sandalwood, tolu, ylang ylang and cinnamon. Sweet, gentle and oriental, like a touch of The Arabian Nights. It is sensuous, warming and earthy in effect and builds self-confidence.

Silk & Satin
A blend featuring karo karounde, iris, vanilla and tuberose. A fine, soft blend with the delightful middle note of tuberose, karo karounde and osmanthus, delicate tones of iris, vanilla and cocoa, with a fruity top note. This harmonious blend has an illuminating effect and operates as a guardian in all situations.
