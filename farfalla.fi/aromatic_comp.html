Aromatic Compositions

 

Symphonies of fragrance...
Our compositions are mixtures made of our own natural essential oils. Fine for special occasions or to create a particular ambience.


Relaxing and balancing...

 

Meditation
A blend of oils including geranium, jasmine, myrrh, neroli
and frankincense. Frankincense, myrrh and jasmine have been used since antiquity for meditation, rituals and eucharists. Our blend gives gentle support on the soul searching journey.

Rainbow
A blend, which includes bergamot, patchouli, rose, vetiver, and ylang ylang. A blend to balance the chakras with the resonance of the seven colours. It creates harmony and balance.

Relaxation
A blend including lavender, chamomile, neroli and mandarin. Bright and slightly spicy in aroma, relaxing, balancing and soothing in effect. The ideal blend to relax and enjoyan evening at home.
