Body Sprays

Wonderful scent companions in a small format. These handy spray bottles have room in every handbag and accompany you through your day. Five scent creations for different situations. Spray a freshness kick on the cleavage, face or the neck and the wrists. Body sprays are made of hydrosols, organic alcohol and a mixture of essential oils.
Comes in 20 ml Bottles

 

Freshen Up
A fruity-cool, freshening spray for warm days and overheated brains. Use: spray once or twice directly on skin. Avoid contact with eyes.
Contents: Orange, lemon, orangeflower water

On the Move
A refreshing spray for concentration while driving or travelling. Use: Spray in the air or on the skin. Avoid contact with eyes.
Contents: lemon, mint, peppermint water

Three Roses
A delicate, pampering flower scent. Use whenever the fancy takes you. Avoid contact with eyes.
Contents: with rose oils and rose water

Wellbeing
A relaxing scent to pamper and cheer. Use: spray once on skin whenever required. Avoid contact with eyes.
Contents: lavendel, orange, neroli
