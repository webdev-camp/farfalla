Application

Air Freshening
Oil burners are suitable for perfuming all types of rooms. Fill the bowl with water, add a few drops of essential oil and light the tealight underneath. Always make sure that there is water in the bowl. Scent stones are suitable for perfuming small rooms, as well as bathrooms, toilets, office desks, bed-side tables, cupboards, cars, etc. How to use: pour 10-15 drops of undiluted oil onto the stone and repeat weekly.

Baths and footbaths
Dosage: 4-10 drops of essential oil per full bath, depending on the oil. Essential oils are not soluble in water and must be mixed with a natural emulsifier (liquid soap, full-cream milk, cream, honey etc.) before use.

Massage and bodycare
Essential oils should not be used undiluted directly on the skin. Dosage: approx. 15-25 drops with 50 ml of fatty oil. Neutral vegetable oils such as sweet almond, jojoba, etc. are suitable.

Inhalation
Dosage: 1-5 drops, depending on the intensity of the oil. Use sparingly, as the oils are very intensive when used in this way.

Cleaning
Add 2-3 drops of an essential oil to a neutral liquid soap and add to the cleaning water. Particularly fresh fragrances like litsea cubeba, lemongrass, citrus fragrances and lavender or lavandin are suitable.

Cosmetics, ointments and in the kitchen
The use of essential oils in cosmetic products and in cookery is described in detail in countless books on these themes. See the specialist literature.
