Oriental Spice
A blend including clove, cinnamon, ginger, cardamom, pepper and bay. Spicy aromas waft from the sacks of cinnamon, cardamom and cloves, which are loaded onto ships in Zanzibar and sent all over the world. This stimulating, purifying blend of spices is eminently suitable for cleansing the air and creating a pure, concentrated working atmosphere.

Shangri La
A blend with frangipani, ylang ylang, vanilla, coconut oil,
cedrat, champac and patchouli. The delicately aromatic frangipani blossoms are the dominant note. Sumptuous ylang ylang and champac flowers are united with the freshness of the cedrat lemon. This precious blend brings harmony, creates a gentle, serene mood, but also has a sensuous, erotic effect.
